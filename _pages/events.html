Arduino Workshops
These workshops teach basic:
  • - Electronics and wiring
  • - Arduino programming and wiring
  • - Use of breadboards, sensors and other parts
The workshops assume no prior knowledge and are run from scratch each semester. Feel free to bring in your own electronics, creations (and problems!) to get help from the students running the workshop after it ends.



The workshops consist of a 1.5 hour segment once per week, generally starting around week 3 and running for at least three weeks. Each session consists both of lecture style theory and hands-on activities where volunteers go around helping people get the tasks done.

Whilst the workshops themselves are free, we ask that everyone brings:
  • - A laptop
  • - An Arduino kit (or your own equivalent parts)
