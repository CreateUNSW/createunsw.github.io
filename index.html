What We Do

Workshops & BBQs

CREATE runs various free workshops & BBQs in both semester 1 and semester 2 of the year. No formal arrangements are necessary to attend the workshops, just show up and join in.

Electronic Component Sales

CREATE sells electronic parts including microcontrollers, breadboards, ardunio kits and quadcopters throughout the semester.

Funding for Student Projects

If you have an idea for a project that involves putting together and making any project, we want to provide you with everything needed to get your project off the ground.
